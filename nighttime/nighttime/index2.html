Solo Night-Time; Activities

Want to tour and see what makes London so special, even at night? Follow one of our guides to take yourself on an adventure around London

Paint Art London

Has pop-up painting events throughout the year – including events for night owls.

London Friday Night Skate

Departs from Wellington Arch at Hyde Park Corner every Friday at 8pm.

Discover

Swingers|Crazy Golf!

Two bespoke nine hole crazy golf courses, five cocktail drenched bars and three of London's best street-food vendors all under one roof. Opens till late in the morning on selected days.

Discover

Ballie Ballerson

looking for evening activities in London, look no further than Ballie Ballerson, the funnest ball pit bar in the capital. Serving up retro candy cocktails and delicious fried foods, the venue also offers a two hour unlimited ball pit session, complete with prosecco and pizza.

Discover

Piano Works West End

Eat, drink and sing your way into the next day at Piano Works West End as their SingEasy evenings brings you a unique dining experience. Request your favourite tunes from the Piano Works live band, and give your own vocal chords a workout in the process..

Discover

Musical Bingo

very month the theme for Musical Bingo changes, so check out when your favourite genre is up for a night of epic songs, great prizes, mystery challenges and tonnes of pizzas.

Discover

Shopping

Some select places in London that are open after hours, for late night shopping. est End, Central London and Kensington High Street stores are usually open until 8pm or 9pm on Thursdays, while Chelsea and Knightsbridge shops stay open late (8pm or 9pm) on Wednesdays.

Discover

Below Zero Ice Bar

It is frozen in its entirety, with all of its contents made of Swedish ice, from the walls, to the seats, tables and even shot glasses.

Discover

The Shard

The tallest building in London – and in Western Europe – but it also offers a truly spectacular 360-degree view of the city and beyond.
